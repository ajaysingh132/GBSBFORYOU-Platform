# GBSBFORYOU Vision

> Official Vision of the GBSBFORYOU Digital Ecosystem

---

# Vision Statement

To build a complete, open, intelligent and collaborative Digital Knowledge Ecosystem that empowers education, publishing, language technology, governance, research and community development for everyone.

---

# Long-Term Vision

GBSBFORYOU aims to become an integrated digital ecosystem where knowledge is created, preserved, shared and continuously improved through technology and collective intelligence.
