# GBSBFORYOU Mission

> Official Mission of the GBSBFORYOU Digital Ecosystem

---

# Mission Statement

Our mission is to develop an open, sustainable, AI-ready and community-driven digital ecosystem that enables knowledge creation, preservation, education, language technology, research, governance and collaboration for everyone.

---

# Core Mission

The GBSBFORYOU Platform serves as the Master Control Center for all ecosystem repositories and projects.

Its mission is to connect people, knowledge, technology and communities through open standards and collaborative development.
